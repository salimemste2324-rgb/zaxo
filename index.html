Our Non-Governmental Vision & Mission (2024-2025)

Vision: To be a modern development, deep-rooted in the patriotic and science-seeking plan, in a productive environment. Mission: 1. Developing the professional capabilities of teachers and enhancing the quality of education. 2. Raising the sense of nationalism and protecting the Kurdish heritage and culture. 3. Modernizing learning methodologies for student success while preserving character/ethics. 4. Focusing on social development and promoting Kurdish identity. 5. Emphasizing student production and innovations.
